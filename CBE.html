Problem and Methodology


Scheduling events across planets is a difficult task. Every planet has a takes a different amount of time to revolve around the sun and due to this they have different day lengths. In order to plan for the time difference, we will need to make a Cosmic Calendar. This Calendar have the ability to take the local time of different planets and convert the time to our own. UTC standard time will be the basis for our calculations to base off of. In order to code this project, we will use the Coder Merlin terminal with HTML, CSS, and Javascript as our languages.
