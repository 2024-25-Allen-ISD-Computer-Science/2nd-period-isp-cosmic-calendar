What is the celestial body Editor

The celestial body editor is a tool used to create a calendar connected to earth’s. We already have a selection of celestial bodies you can pick, but space is infinite. Since we can’t have every planet, asteroid, or anything floating in space. We made the celestial body editor to help you. All you do is put the data that the questions are asking. After pressing Calculate it will take you to a new page exactly like our main page but with the data you gave us.
